Hi, I’m Pavel.

Professionally, I do frontend web development. I currently work as an accessibility lead at Squarespace, trying to ensure that the company’s products work well for the widest audience, regardless of people’s abilities, preferences, and circumstances.

Brief History

I started working as a web developer in 2013, going through a series of small startups, usually being the only web frontend engineer in the company.

One of the startups got acquired by Samsung, where I stayed from 2017 to 2019. Then I left for Squarespace.

Design and Technology

Web design and web engineering are a continuum rather than two distinct disciplines. A great experience can only be born if both the user needs and the web medium itself are deeply understood, and if the interface is structurally sound and visually harmonious.

I think of myself as an engineer who can tell good design from bad, especially on the Web. I appreciate user-centric design, and I often find myself helping designers put user needs above their creative ambitions.
